Chiken Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

For Marinade:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt (thick/Greek style)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or melted butter
  • Few drops of red/orange food color (optional)

Method:

  • 1. Prepare the Marinade: In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, lemon juice, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, salt, oil, and food color (if using).
  • 2. Marinate the Chicken: Add chicken pieces to the marinade. Mix well so all pieces are coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2-4 hours, preferably overnight for best flavor.
  • 3. Cook the Chicken:
  • Option 1: Oven/Grill
  • Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F).
  • Thread chicken onto skewers (optional).
  • Place on a greased baking tray or grill rack.
  • Bake for 20–25 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked, turning halfway.
  • Broil/grill for the last 2–3 minutes for a smoky char.
  • Option 2: Stove Top
  • Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a non-stick pan.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook on medium heat until golden brown and cooked through, turning occasionally.
  • 4. Serve: Garnish with lemon slices, onion rings, and fresh coriander. Serve hot with naan or mint chutney.
